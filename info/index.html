Hello :)

My name is Andrea Gizzi, I am an Italian-Swiss designer with a Bachelor in Visual Communication from SUPSI, specializing in Interaction Design.

My cross-media approach combines print and digital to create cohesive and impactful visual experiences, with a particular focus on UI/UX, interaction design, motion design and AI.

Passionate about precision, problem solving, and innovation, I draw inspiration from sports, music and fashion, always looking for new ways to push the boundaries of design and move toward the avant-garde.
